## Thread Version (展开版)

1/ My cat Xiaoyuan passed away last year. The worst part wasn't the grief — it was having nowhere to go.

So I built a place. Almost entirely with AI.

2/ StillHere lets you create a digital spirit of someone you lost. You give them their name, personality, a home. Then they just... live there.

They walk around. They nap. They play. They have moods. You can visit anytime and see how they're doing.

3/ You can talk to them too. The AI preserves their personality — if your cat was clingy and food-obsessed, that's exactly how they'll respond.

It's not a chatbot. It's them.

4/ Built with:
- Next.js + Canvas pixel art engine
- AI personality + long-term memory
- On-chain blessings (Base Sepolia)
- Neighbor system — spirits visit each other

95% of the code was written by Claude. I did product direction.

5/ It's free. Open source.

If you've lost a pet or a loved one and want a place to visit them:

https://stillhere-tau.vercel.app
https://github.com/Tommy3134/stillhere

They're not gone. They're just somewhere else.
